What is SWARM? 🐝

SWARM is a social pricing network where collective buying power forces retail prices to collapse.

How it works:

  1. Start a Tag: Spot a product on Amazon, eBay, or anywhere else. Share it here with its original retail price and pitch a massive target discount.
  2. Set the Target: Decide how many committed buyers (the Swarm) are required to legally force that discount.
  3. Pledge & Collapse: Users who want the item commit (pledge) to buy it only if the target is hit. Once the crowd size triggers the goal, the price drops and the deal is unlocked for everyone!

Power to the crowd. Drop prices together.
